Features

Discover intelligent features crafted to revolutionize your campus dining experience, making every meal convenient, efficient, and perfectly timed.

  • 🍕

    Quick Ordering

    Transform mealtime frustration into effortless satisfaction. Browse every campus restaurant in one unified app, customize your order exactly how you like it, and place it with just a few intuitive taps – all while walking to your next class.

  • ⏰

    Schedule Pickup

    Time is your most valuable resource on campus. Select your perfect pickup time that syncs seamlessly with your academic schedule. Arrive precisely when your fresh, hot meal is ready – no more counting minutes in endless lines.

  • 🏫

    Campus-Wide Access

    Your entire campus dining ecosystem at your fingertips. From the main dining hall to that hidden café behind the library – access every restaurant, and eatery across campus through one powerful, comprehensive platform.

  • ❤️

    Save Favorites

    Create your personal culinary shortcuts with favorites that track your preferences. Build custom quick-order combinations, save go-to meals for busy exam weeks, and reorder your comfort foods with lightning speed.

  • 💳

    Easy Payments

    Experience frictionless transactions designed for student life. Seamlessly use UPI payments with secure, one-tap checkout that keeps you moving forward.

  • 📅

    Future Scheduling

    Master your meal planning like a pro student. Schedule orders days or weeks in advance for important study sessions, group meetings, or special campus events. Never again worry about finding time to eat during your busiest academic schedule.
